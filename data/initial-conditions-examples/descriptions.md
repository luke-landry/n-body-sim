# Descriptions of example initial conditions

## ic-circle-2-body
2-body system where the bodies move in a perfect circle around a shared center point.
| G | Time Step | Softening Factor|
|---|---|---|
| 1 | 0.01 | 0.005 |

## ic-figure-eight
3-body system where the bodies move in a figure-eight pattern. 

The initial condition values were found from this video:
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=iuRtNK2-jO8 

| G | Time Step | Softening Factor|
|---|---|---|
| 1 | 0.01 | 0.005 |
